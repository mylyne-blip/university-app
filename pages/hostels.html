Student Hostels

Uungano University provides comfortable, secure, and modern hostel facilities for our students with various room options and world-class amenities.

Hostel Features

Modern Rooms

Well-furnished single, double, and triple rooms with comfortable beds, study desks, and storage facilities

High-Speed WiFi

Free high-speed internet connectivity in all rooms and common areas for seamless online learning

Common Areas

Recreational lounges, TV rooms, game areas, and meeting spaces for socializing and group studies

Dining Facilities

On-site cafeteria with healthy, nutritious meals served three times daily with dietary preferences catered

24/7 Security

Professional security personnel, CCTV surveillance, and secure entry/exit systems for student safety

Medical Center

On-site medical facilities with trained healthcare professionals available 24/7 for emergency care

Laundry Services

Professional laundry services available at minimal cost or self-service laundry facilities

Transportation

Free shuttle services to campus, city center, and major transportation hubs for student convenience

Hostel Rules & Regulations

  • Check-in time: 2:00 PM | Check-out time: 11:00 AM
  • Quiet hours: 10:00 PM - 7:00 AM (guests must maintain noise discipline)
  • No smoking or alcohol permitted inside hostel premises
  • Visitors allowed only during designated hours with proper identification
  • Room inspection conducted monthly for cleanliness and maintenance
  • Cooking not permitted in rooms; only designated common kitchen available
  • Damage to hostel property will be charged to the student's account

How to Apply for Hostel

  1. Submit hostel application form with your admission letter
  2. Pay hostel deposit (refundable upon departure)
  3. Wait for hostel allocation confirmation
  4. Receive hostel assignment and check-in schedule
  5. Complete hostel orientation and settle in

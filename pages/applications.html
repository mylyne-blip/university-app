How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to apply to Uungano University and start your journey towards academic excellence.

  1. Create Your Account - Visit our online portal and register with your email address
  2. Fill Application Form - Complete the detailed application form with your academic and personal information
  3. Upload Documents - Submit required documents (transcripts, certificates, test scores)
  4. Pay Application Fee - Submit a non-refundable application fee of $50 USD
  5. Submit Application - Review all information and submit your complete application
  6. Entrance Exam/Interview - Qualified candidates will be invited for entrance examination or interview
  7. Wait for Decision - Admissions decisions are made within 4-6 weeks of application
  8. Enrollment - Accepted students complete enrollment and pay tuition fees

Required Documents

Academic Records: High school transcripts or equivalent
Identification: Valid passport or national ID copy
Test Scores: SAT/ACT scores or university entrance exam results
Language Test: TOEFL/IELTS for international students (if required)
Recommendation Letters: 2-3 letters from teachers or professors
Personal Statement: Essay explaining your academic goals and interests

Application Deadlines

Fall Intake: Applications open January 1 - Deadline: June 30
Spring Intake: Applications open July 1 - Deadline: December 31
Summer Intake: Available for select programs - Deadline: May 15

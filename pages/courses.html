Our Courses

Uungano University offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs designed to meet the needs of today's job market and tomorrow's challenges.

Engineering

4-year Bachelor's program in innovative engineering disciplines including Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, and Software Engineering.

Duration: 4 years | Intake: 200+ students annually

Business

MBA and Bachelor programs in business management, accounting, finance, and entrepreneurship with practical industry exposure.

Duration: 3-4 years | Intake: 150+ students annually

Sciences

Programs in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics with emphasis on research and practical laboratory work.

Duration: 3-4 years | Intake: 250+ students annually

Humanities

Liberal arts and social sciences programs in Literature, History, Philosophy, and Political Science.

Duration: 3 years | Intake: 180+ students annually

Information Technology

Cutting-edge IT programs covering software development, data science, cybersecurity, and cloud computing.

Duration: 4 years | Intake: 200+ students annually

Health Sciences

Medical and health-related programs including Medicine, Nursing, Public Health, and Pharmacy.

Duration: 4-6 years | Intake: 120+ students annually

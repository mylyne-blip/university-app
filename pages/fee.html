Tuition & Fees

Transparent and competitive pricing for high-quality education. Financial aid and scholarships are available for qualified students.

Tuition by Student Category

Domestic Undergraduates: ksh 8,000 per semester
Domestic Postgraduates: ksh 6,500 per semester
International Undergraduates: ksh 10,000 per semester
International Postgraduates: ksh 15,000 per semester

Additional Fees

Registration Fee: ksh 1000 (one-time, non-refundable)
Student ID & Card: ksh 250 per academic year
Library & Laboratory: ksh 3000 per semester
Sports & Recreation: ksh 2000 per semester
Health Insurance: ksh 4000 per year
Technology Fee: ksh 1500 per semester

Hostel & Accommodation

Single Room: $2,000 per semester
Double Room: $1,500 per semester
Triple Room: $1,200 per semester
Off-Campus Housing Allowance: $1,800 per semester

Payment Plans & Scholarships

We offer flexible payment options and multiple scholarship opportunities:

  • Merit-based scholarships (covering 25%-100% tuition)
  • Need-based financial aid
  • Sports scholarships
  • Installment payment plans (interest-free)
  • Corporate and government scholarships
  • International student scholarships
